**Do’s**

* Think before posting – Make sure your post is respectful and appropriate.
* Protect your privacy – Keep personal information like phone number, address, and bank details private.
* Use strong passwords – Enable two-factor authentication for social media accounts.
* Verify information before sharing – Check if news or information is fake or real before reposting.
* Maintain a professional online image – Employers and professors may check your social media profiles.
* Respect others online – Use polite language and avoid online arguments.
* Give credit to original creators when sharing their content.



**Don’ts**

* Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
* Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
* Don’t spread fake news or unverified information.
* Don’t cyberbully or troll others online.
* Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people.
* Don’t post when you are angry or emotional.
* Don’t share your location publicly in real time.
